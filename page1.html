In this case I indicated HumanoidRootPart as starting position

Properties:

Name - this is the name of your hitbox

StartCFrame - this is the starting position of your hitbox

ForwardCFrame - This is how far your hitbox will be from the StartCFrame (-1 will be in front of you, 1 will be behind you).

Size - This is the size of your hitbox (specify in Vector3)

Duration - This is the delay before your hitbox disappears.

Visible - will your hitbox be visible (true or false)

Rotate - This is whether your hitbox will be rotated in one of three directions (specify in Vector3)

Char - It must be indicated to avoid further consequences.
